About Bounce House Uganda

Best Bounce Houses in Uganda

Welcome to Uganda Bounce House Rentals, your premier destination for bounce houses, inflatable slides, and combo units. We've streamlined the rental process, making it easy and convenient for you. At Uganda Bounce House Rentals LLC, we prioritize your safety and enjoyment. Our team of professionals is fully insured and SIOTO certified.

How to Order

Booking is simple. Choose your preferred date and unit, and Uganda Bounce House Rentals LLC will handle the rest. Once you've completed the initial commitment Fee (Mobile Money), you'll receive a confirmation email and with your receipt. Expect a call from one of our owners to confirm your appointment and address any queries. If online payment is inconvenient, call us at +256 701-299-127 for alternative arrangements.

Delivery Process

On the agreed delivery day, our team will arrive within the specified window to set up your unit. We'll provide training on usage, and you'll be required to sign our rental agreement. After setup, you have full control. The rental period is 7 hours, with an additional 2-3 hours for pickup. We'll inspect and clean the unit during pickup. Any damage may incur a replacement cost.

Preparation Tips

Clear a 20' x 20' area for installation, free of debris. Mow the grass, especially for wet/dry slides, which require a larger area. Ensure no obstacles block the installation area. A 110-volt power outlet within 100 feet is needed. If unavailable, we offer generators for rent. Notify us if installing on a hard surface, and inform about sprinkler systems or use of sandbags instead of stakes. For park use, obtain approval from the park beforehand.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations can be made up to 2 weeks before delivery, with a returned deposit (minus non-refundable fees). Within two weeks, a $60 cancellation fee applies, which can be applied to a future rental.

Safety Measures

Safety is our top priority. We reserve the right to cancel in winds exceeding 18 MPH or unsuitable conditions. Inclement weather can lead to cancellation, with refunds or rescheduling. Evacuate the inflatable in case of rain, lightning, or strong winds. Remain calm in power outages, exit the unit, and check connections. Silly String is prohibited, and a $100 cleanup fee applies. Report any damage promptly.

Unit Capacities:

  • Under 7: 10-12 children
  • 8 – 12: 7 – 10 persons
  • Over 12: 5 – 7 persons

Weight restriction: 200 lbs per person max. Maximum of 2 people at a time on the slide, of comparable weight, height, & size.
